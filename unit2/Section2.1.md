3. Sets of numbers
a) Natrual numbers: $\mathbb{N}=\{0, 1, 2, 3, 4, ...\}$
b) Integers: $\mathbb{Z}=\{...,-2,-1,0,1,2,...\}$
c) Positive Integers: $\mathbb{Z}^+=\{1, 2, 3, 4, ...\}$
d) Rational numbers: $\mathbb{Q}=\{m/n\in\mathbb{Z}, n\neq0\}$
e) Real numbers: $\mathbb{R}$

others: $\mathbb{Q}^+$,$\mathbb{R}^-$, ...
